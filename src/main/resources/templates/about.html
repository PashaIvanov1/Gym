Atlant Fitness Club

Equipped with the most modern strength and cardio equipment from Body-Solid and Johnson (USA).

Workouts in our fitness club will provide excellent muscle tone, significantly improve body contours, and help you get rid of your problem areas.

Remember, for proper training, it is necessary to combine cardio exercises with strength work. For this purpose, your fitness program must include engaging exercises on machines and various free weight exercises.

Types of Equipment

  • Cardio machines: equipment for strengthening the cardiovascular system, improving endurance, and burning fat.
  • Elliptical trainer: develops muscles in the upper and lower body.
  • Treadmill: develops leg muscles and aids in cardiovascular training.
  • Horizontal exercise bike: provides back support and muscle development.
  • Vertical exercise bike: engages all leg muscles.
  • Strength equipment: machines for developing specific muscle groups.
  • Free weights - dumbbells, barbells: primary load for muscles.

Additional Amenities

In our fitness club, you can exercise independently or under the supervision of a qualified trainer.

For visitors, the club is equipped with air conditioners to keep you cool. There is also a water cooler to quench your thirst and satellite television.

You have the opportunity to store your belongings in personal lockers during your workout and take a leisurely shower after your session.
