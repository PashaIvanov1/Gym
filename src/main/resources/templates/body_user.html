Weekly Workout Plan

The presented workout program is an example of a triple split for mass gain in the gym. In other words, the program involves three powerful strength workouts per week, each targeting a specific muscle group. It should be noted that this mass gain workout program is more suitable for advanced mesomorph athletes rather than beginners, as it includes not only basic exercises but also isolating exercises. Additionally, the program requires a high level of endurance and gym training experience.

Day 1

Day 1: Chest, Biceps, Legs

  • The first day of the workout program focuses on two major muscle groups of the body — chest and legs. It should be noted that if you have the opportunity to go to the gym four times a week, it's better to allocate a separate leg workout.

Day 2

Day 2: Shoulders, Triceps

  • The second day of the workout program focuses on the upper half of the body. The goal is to work the muscles that give the body mass. Well-developed and round shoulders will make your figure broader, while a well-developed triceps will increase the size of your arms, visually widening the biceps.

Day 3

Day 3: Back and Abs

  • The final day of the training focuses on working out the back and abs. Upper back exercises like lat pulldowns and pull-ups develop the latissimus dorsi muscles, while dumbbell rows and seated cable rows add thickness to the back. The program may also include deadlifts.
