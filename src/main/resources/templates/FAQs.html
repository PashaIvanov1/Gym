FAQs

What should I bring to the gym?

You should bring a change of clothes, sneakers, clean underwear. Optionally, you can bring workout gloves, a towel for showering, and shower slippers.

How much does a single personal training session cost?

The cost of a single individual training session is 90 UAH.

If I have a spinal injury, will the trainer take it into account when designing the program?

Yes, the trainer takes into account injuries and health conditions when designing the program.

Tell me about the "Candy Day" promotion.

Within 5 days from your birthday, you have the opportunity to get a 50% discount on a membership at our fitness club.

We will need any copy of a document that confirms your date of birth.

Can I exercise at your gym after an injury?

Yes, the competence of our trainers allows them to create a program for people in the rehabilitation and post-rehabilitation period.
